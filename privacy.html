Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Toklify ("we") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website (the "Toklify" service).

1. Information We Collect

Toklify is committed to minimizing data collection. We do not ask you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email, or phone number. However, our servers automatically log standard technical data such as IP addresses, browser types, and access times for security and internal analytics purposes.

2. Third-Party Links

Our service relies on third-party APIs (such as TikWM) to process videos. When you enter a TikTok URL, that URL is forwarded to these third-party servers for retrieval. We do not control the privacy practices of these third parties and recommend reading their privacy policies.

3. Data Storage

We do not store the videos you download on our servers. The download process occurs directly between your device and the video source. The download history you see on the site is stored locally in your browser (LocalStorage) and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Advertising

We may use third-party advertising (such as Google AdSense) to support the operation of this site. These ad providers may use cookies to personalize ads. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@toklify.com.
