Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find quick answers to common questions about using Toklify.

Is Toklify really free?
Yes, Toklify is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, no subscriptions required, and no daily limits on the number of videos you can download. We display light ads to support server maintenance.
Will the downloaded videos have the TikTok watermark?
No. By default, all videos processed via the "Download Without Watermark" button will be clean of the TikTok logo and creator username. You will get the raw video in its original quality.
Do I need to install an app or software?
Not at all. Toklify is a web-based service. You only need a browser (like Chrome, Safari, or Firefox) to access the site and download videos. No additional app installation is required.
Are my downloaded videos stored on Toklify's servers?
No. Your privacy is our priority. We do not store, copy, or keep backups of the videos you download on our servers. The download process happens directly between TikTok's servers and your device.
How do I download a TikTok video as an MP3 file?
After you paste the TikTok video link and processing is complete, you will see several download format options. Click the "Audio MP3" button to extract and download the sound from the video separately.
Can Toklify download videos from private accounts?
No. We respect content creators' privacy settings. Toklify can only process and download videos from public TikTok accounts. Videos from private accounts cannot be accessed by our system.
Will the TikTok creator know if I download their video?
No. The download process via Toklify is anonymous. Our system fetches the video data directly without connecting to your TikTok account. Creators will not receive any notifications when you download their videos.
Why is there an ad when choosing 1080p quality?
To keep this service free without spamming ads across the entire page, we only show a single short ad when users choose to download in the highest resolution (Full HD). Server costs for processing large files are expensive, and ad support helps us maintain this feature.
Can Toklify be used on Android phones and iPhones (iOS)?
Yes, Toklify is designed to be fully responsive and works perfectly on all mobile devices. Whether you use Android, iPhone, or a tablet, the site layout will adapt, and the download process will run smoothly.
What should I do if the video fails to download?
If the download fails, ensure your internet connection is stable. Try refreshing the page and pasting the link again. If the issue persists, the video may have been deleted by the creator or reported, making it inaccessible from TikTok's servers.

Tips & Troubleshooting

Overcome common issues and maximize your downloading experience.

The download button opens a new tab instead of saving directly
This happens because your browser settings block automatic downloads. You can press "Save" (or Ctrl+S / Cmd+S) on the new tab. For iPhone Safari users, press and hold the video that appears, then select "Download Linked File".
Cannot paste the link from the clipboard
Make sure you have granted "Paste" permissions for this site in your browser settings. Alternatively, you can click the "Paste from clipboard" button or use keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V) directly in the input box.
The downloaded video quality looks blurry
The downloaded video quality depends on the original quality uploaded by the creator. If the creator uploaded a low-resolution video, our system cannot upscale it to HD. Always choose the "1080p Full HD" button to get the best available quality.
Tip: How to copy a link quickly
In the TikTok app, you don't need to wait for the video to finish playing. Just tap the "Share" button on the right side of the screen, then select "Copy link". The link will be instantly saved to your phone's clipboard, ready to be pasted into Toklify.
